gstack 调试与安全模式栈 - 手绘框架图

Single-page hand-drawn educational framework diagram on warm cream paper.
Black hand-drawn lines with slight wobble, soft pastel color blocks. Simple,
friendly, diagram-style visuals ONLY.

PALETTE: macaron — soft pastel blocks on warm cream
COLORS: Warm Cream background (#F5F0E8); Black (#1A1A1A) for ALL lines, text,
        arrows, and doodles; Light Blue (#A8D8EA), Mint Green (#B5E5CF),
        Lavender (#D5C6E0), Peach (#FFD5C2) for section fills;
        Coral Red (#E8656D) for warning/emphasis accents only.
        Color values and names are rendering guidance only — do NOT display
        color names, hex codes, or palette labels as visible text.

STRUCTURE: Layered shield / pyramid framework, read from bottom to top.

LAYERS (bottom → top):
- BOTTOM LAYER: A large rounded rectangle labeled "/investigate" with a
  magnifying-glass-over-code icon. Represents root-cause debugging.
  Fill: Light Blue (#A8D8EA).
- MIDDLE LAYER: Three adjacent rounded cards sitting on the bottom layer:
  * Left card: "/careful" with a warning-triangle icon (Coral Red accent)
  * Center card: "/freeze" with an ice-cube icon plus a dashed boundary line
  * Right card: "/unfreeze" with a melting-key icon
  Fills: Mint, Lavender, Peach.
- TOP LAYER: A shield-shaped banner labeled "/guard" spanning across the three
  middle cards. Small icons from the three middle cards repeat inside the shield.
  Fill: slightly darker pastel mix or Coral Red outline for emphasis.

CONNECTIONS: Thin hand-drawn arrows show that /guard combines /careful + /freeze
             (and /unfreeze releases the freeze). Small Chinese annotations:
             "组合" (combine) and "解除" (release).

ELEMENTS: Rounded cards with wobbly borders, simple sketchy icons, hand-drawn
          arrows, small doodle stars and underlines. Clean composition with
          generous white space.

STYLE: Minimal, well-organized, airy. Hand-lettered Chinese text — no computer
       fonts. Short labels and keywords only.

TEXT: Large and prominent with handwritten-style fonts. Keep minimal. All text
      in Chinese.

COMPLEXITY: medium
